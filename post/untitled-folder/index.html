【運科竹學術生涯新篇章：感謝與期待】

很榮幸在這個春季，獲聘擔任台北城市科技大學休閒事業系的兼任講師。

這段時間以來，從運動科學的學術研究到第一線的實務教學，我始終致力於將複雜的科學知識轉化為具備實踐價值的應用技術。這次將負責授課《健身教練指導》與《重量訓練指導法》，這兩門課結合了我過往的專業認證與訓練心得，希望能將這些知識傳遞給未來的產業生力軍。

感謝過去所有師長的提攜與這一路走來支持「我是運科竹」的你們。這不僅是一個講師職位的開始，更是我實踐「運動科學普及化」理想的另一個起點。期待在教室裡，與同學們一同探索運動訓練的奧秘！

從碩士班到現在的博士候選人，我的學習之路一直圍繞著「如何讓身體動得更有效率? 以及如何透過身體活動來增進認知功能？」這一核心。過去在研究室與實驗室的數據中打滾，與在健身房中面對不同訓練者的需求，這兩者的結合構成了我的教學理念。

這次受聘為講師，讓我重新檢視了自己的知識體系。如何用簡潔明瞭的方式引導學生？如何將專業的「肌力與體能」轉化為「能被執行的教練技術」？這是我在接下來課程中給自己的課題。教書，本質上也是另一種形式的科學傳播，希望能與學生們教學相長，在休閒事業系開創不一樣的教學火花。

[A New Chapter in Sports Science Education]

I am honored to announce my appointment as an Adjunct Lecturer in the Department of Leisure Management at Taipei City University of Science and Technology this semester.

Bridging the gap between empirical sports science and real-world application has always been the cornerstone of my professional journey. Teaching “Fitness Instructor Advising” and “Guide for Weight Training” is a fantastic opportunity to translate my research background and certification expertise into actionable skills for the next generation of industry professionals.

I am deeply grateful for the mentorship I’ve received and the ongoing support from the community following “Coach Steve’s Performance.” This position represents more than just an academic appointment; it is a significant milestone in my commitment to making sports science accessible and practical. I look forward to an enriching semester of inquiry and growth with my students.

My academic trajectory has been fundamentally defined by one central question: How can we optimize human movement? & How can we improve our cognitive function through physical activity? Navigating through both the rigorous environment of research laboratories and the dynamic space of the gym floor has profoundly shaped my pedagogical approach.

Stepping into this role as a lecturer has provided a valuable opportunity to refine my expertise. My primary objective is to distill complex concepts—ranging from kinesiology to biomechanics—into practical, instruction-oriented techniques that students can confidently apply in their future careers. To me, teaching is an extension of scientific communication. I am eager to foster an interactive learning environment where, together with my students, we can elevate the standard of sports and fitness education.
